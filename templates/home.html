About EDUSCHEME

EDUSCHEME is a scholarship discovery platform designed to help students easily find government scholarships, fellowships, and welfare schemes. Many students miss valuable opportunities simply because they are unaware of them. This portal helps students explore schemes based on education level, income category, gender, disability status and more.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a centralized digital platform where students across the country can quickly discover and access government scholarships and welfare schemes without navigating multiple complicated portals.
